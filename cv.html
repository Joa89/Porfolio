ABOUT ME

A small introduction about myself

Hi there!

I am a beginner frondend developer with strong motivation to turn my passion into a way for living.

I love to see the results of my work. If I was born in the Middle Age, I would definitely be some kind of craftsmen. But last time I've checked it was a XXI century, so in these times web technologies seems to be a better choice.

I have a formal economic education which means I'm taking care of details, I have a strong problem solving attitude and I'm good in communication especially with people who know nothing about what I really do :) Unexpectedly, all those things make me a better frontend developer (and probably a better human as well).

After gaining some experience in administration and financial areas I started to feel like a full-time guardian of the past. It took me some time to realize that I would rather be in the team of people who create the future, but finally here I am, ready to make changes which matter!

Anyway, I'm glad you are here and hope you enjoy this small introducion about myself. Below you can get familiar with technologies I use in my projects and my porfolio. After that if you have any questions just go to the contact and feel free to chat with me.
